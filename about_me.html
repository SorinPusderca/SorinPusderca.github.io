1985 Suzuki Intruder VS750 G.

The motorcycle was originally built in Japan and intended for export in Canada. In the summer of 2019, I bought it from the Netherlands. Back then I didn't know exactly what I was buying, after researching the motorcycle I found out that the G type was produced only in 1985 and comes from the factory equipped with the 45-degree V-twin VR51B engine that develops 63 horsepower. Starting with 1986, her sisters GLP/GLF received the same engine with only 55 horsepower with and some lucky GL's and F variants reciving the 63 horsepower engine.

She had a hard life until I bought her. All is not lost with this one, in the fall of 2019, a mechanical and tinsmith intervention took place. Being "isolated" in a town. I downloaded the repair manual from the internet and ordered the necessary parts.. from Japan. With a lot of care and mechanical knowledge combined with sleepless nights, 4 weeks later and a new shade of paint, new shotgun exhaust pips she was ready to blast through the streets. It was challenging but I managed, alone, to bring her back to life.
